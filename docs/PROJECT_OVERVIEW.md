# Evergreen Market — Project Overview

## What this project is

Evergreen Market is a local storefront connected to the Evergreen coffee shop in Kyiv.

The coffee shop already buys products directly from suppliers for daily work. The marketplace lets nearby residents order selected products for home use and pick them up at Evergreen.

## Customer-facing explanation

Evergreen Market should be explained simply:

> Ми закуповуємо товари для кавʼярні напряму у постачальників. Частину цих товарів ви можете замовити для себе і забрати в Evergreen.

## Main goals

- Make useful everyday products available to nearby residents.
- Explain clearly where products come from.
- Explain supplier minimum orders without sounding bureaucratic.
- Keep pickup simple and transparent.
- Prepare the business for possible future local delivery.

## Current fulfillment model

- Main fulfillment method: pickup at Evergreen coffee shop.
- Delivery is not active unless explicitly enabled in the code/business settings.
- Future local delivery may be mentioned only as a future plan.

## Voice

The project should feel:

- local;
- warm;
- honest;
- simple;
- helpful;
- transparent.

Avoid sounding like a corporate marketplace, wholesale platform, or legal service.

## Language

All public customer-facing UI copy should be Ukrainian.

Developer notes may be English, Ukrainian, or Russian, but new storefront text should not be Russian unless explicitly requested.
